ENERGY DRINK INGREDIENTS

At Omega Energy, we believe that a successful business should go hand in hand with a responsible attitude towards the environment. We strive to minimize our impact on the planet and actively support nature conservation initiatives. Learn more about our efforts to protect the environment.

Water

The foundation of our drink is the purest artesian water, providing essential hydration and serving as the perfect solvent for other ingredients.

Natural Caffeine

We use natural caffeine, derived from green coffee beans, to provide a sustained energy boost and enhance concentration.

Guarana Extract

Guarana is a natural source of caffeine that enhances the energy effect and helps improve physical endurance.

Natural Flavors

We add natural fruit and berry flavors to give our drink a refreshing and unique taste.

Sweeteners (Natural or Low-Calorie)

To give the drink a pleasant sweetness, we use natural sweeteners or their low-calorie alternatives, so you can enjoy the taste without extra calories.

Taurine

Taurine is an amino acid that plays an important role in the body's energy processes and helps improve cognitive functions.

Vitamin Complex (B3, B6, B12)

The complex of B vitamins helps maintain normal energy metabolism, reduces fatigue, and contributes to the improvement of the nervous system.
