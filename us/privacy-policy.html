PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Omega Energy ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website (the "Website") or purchase our energy drinks (the "Products").

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information from you, including:

  • Personal Information: This is information that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly, such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, date of birth (to verify legal drinking age), payment information (credit card details, etc.), and any other information you voluntarily provide to us.
  • Non-Personal Information: This is information that does not directly identify you, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, website usage data (pages visited, time spent on the Website, etc.), and aggregated information.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Directly from You: We collect personal information when you:
    • Create an account on our Website.
    • Place an order for our Products.
    • Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications.
    • Contact us through our Website or other channels.
    • Participate in surveys, contests, or promotions.
    • Submit reviews or other content on our Website.
  • Automatically: We collect certain non-personal information automatically when you interact with our Website through the use of cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies. These technologies help us understand your Browse behavior and improve our Website.
  • From Other Sources: We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as social media platforms (if you connect your account to our Website) and marketing partners.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Process and Fulfill Your Orders: This includes processing payments, shipping your Products, and providing order updates.
  • To Provide Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, address your concerns, and provide assistance with our Products or Website.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To understand your preferences and tailor our content, recommendations, and offers to your interests.
  • To Send You Marketing Communications: With your consent, we may send you emails or other communications about new products, promotions, and other updates from Omega Energy. You can opt-out of these communications at any time.
  • To Improve Our Website and Products: To analyze website usage data, identify trends, and make improvements to our Website's functionality and user experience, as well as to enhance our Products.
  • To Detect and Prevent Fraud and Abuse: To protect our business and customers from fraudulent activities and misuse of our Website.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • For Other Legitimate Business Purposes: Such as data analysis, research, and development.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with the following categories of recipients:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us with various aspects of our business operations, such as payment processing, shipping and delivery, website hosting, email marketing, data analytics, and customer service. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Marketing Partners: With your consent, we may share your information with marketing partners to send you promotional materials or offers that may be of interest to you.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information to government authorities, law enforcement agencies, or other third parties if we are required to do so by law or legal process, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or other business transaction, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity or third party involved. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our Website if such a transfer occurs and your personal information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
  • Aggregated and Anonymized Information: We may share aggregated and anonymized information that does not directly identify you with third parties for various purposes, including marketing and analytics.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You have certain rights and choices regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You may have the right to access and request corrections to the personal information we hold about you. You can typically review and update your account information through our Website.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You can opt-out of receiving marketing emails from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the emails or by contacting us directly.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: You can manage your preferences regarding cookies and other tracking technologies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Website.
  • Data Deletion: In certain circumstances, you may have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. Please contact us to submit such a request. We may need to retain certain information for legal or legitimate business purposes.
  • Do Not Track: Our Website does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include the use of encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer (SSL) technology. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

7. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Website and Products are intended for adults of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the legal drinking age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.
